Dr. Minhal Hasham, co-founder

We are a team of chemistry PhDs and software engineers with a background in academic research, green chemistry, and systems thinking who are dedicated to fostering equitable, systemic change in chemistry research. Our extensive experience and interactions with scientists from both the Global South and Global North have allowed us to witness first-hand the profound systemic inequities that shape scientific research in chemistry. Our goal is to contribute to the dismantling of these barriers and participate in the building of a more equitable and just academic system in chemistry. We aim to reach this goal by developing and reinforcing relationships between key research stakeholders in the Global South and the Global North. Our objective is to develop a program targeting the equitable generation of knowledge and scientific innovations.
